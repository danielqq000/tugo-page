About Tugo Teahouse

Our Story

Welcome to Tugo Teahouse, your destination for authentic Taiwanese cuisine and premium bubble tea in Arlington Heights, Illinois. We bring you the flavors of Taiwan with our carefully crafted dishes and beverages, using traditional recipes and the finest ingredients.

From our signature beef noodle soup to our handcrafted boba drinks, every item on our menu is prepared with passion and attention to detail. Whether you're craving a hearty meal or a refreshing bubble tea, we're here to provide you with an authentic taste of Taiwan.
