About Tugo Teahouse

Our Name — Tugo Tea House

Our intent was to incorporate a name fitting for our Tea House that not only had links to family heritage in Taiwan, but a name that was easy for our guests to remember and share.

We chose and adapted the name "Tugo" (Tugo Tea House) as the primary link to Jessica's family great ancestral hometown Northeastern Tainan County, Taiwan. The Village name is pronounced 土溝 — Tǔ gōu. We took the liberty to shorten the spelling to adapt to the local market in America and specially Chicagoland and to make the name a play on words (Tugo/To Go).

For reference, Tugou is Northeast of Houbi District and Northwest of Baihe District and Southwest of Chiayi City in a vast and rich agricultural countryside area. Where the locals are exceptionally warm hearted, inviting, and friendly.
